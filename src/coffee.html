COFFEE

We work with specialty coffeefrom current crops which is carefully roasted. Our coffee is fruity, juicy and never boring. We bring you the best from leading european specialty roasteries, currently mostly from Square Mile Coffee Roasters, a multi award winning coffeeroasting company based in East London.

You can currently buy these beans in 350g bags

QUEJINA (GUATEMALA)

redcurrant/honeysuckle/juicy

Fruity redcurrant and sweet honeysuckle flavours. An incredibly juicy coffee which we struggled to decide on the right berry descriptor, but rasberry, blackberry and blueberry were also in the running!
